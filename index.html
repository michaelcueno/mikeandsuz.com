Travel

We picked Sayulita for it's laid back, surfer beach town vibes.



Sayulita is 40 minutes away from the Puerto Vallarta International Airport.

Stay

We have a hotel block at the Amor Boutique Hotel. We think the weekend would be most special if everyone could stay in the same place.



One word of caution! The hotel doesn't have any elevators and some of the villas are pretty high off sea level - so if you're not up for hiking up a few stairs, make sure to pick a room or villa closer to ground level. (The hotel website will tell you how many stairs it takes to get to each room).
