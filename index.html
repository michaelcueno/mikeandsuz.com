Travel / Transportation

We picked Sayulita, Mexico for it's laid back, surfer beach town vibes.



How do I get there from the airport?
Sayulita is a 40 minute drive from the Puerto Vallarta International Airport. There are a few options to get there:

  • Taxi - Taxis only take cash, so make sure to have some on hand. They will take US Dollars but you're likely to get overcharged. If you can, best to exchange some money for Pesos when you arrive at the Airport. Expect to pay somewhere between 30-50 USD for a Taxi.
  • Private Shuttle - You can book a private Shuttle to pick you up from the airport. This is a good option if you have a group or just want someone to hand you a beer as soon as you get on the road. We've used Sayulita Suburban in the past, and they're great. You can also book a shuttle directly through the hotel by emailing info@amorboutiquehotel.com.
  • Uber - Uber does operate in Sayulita and Puerto Vallarta, but they are not allowed in the airport. You'll need to walk outside of the airport if you want to take an Uber. We recommend using a Taxi or Shuttle from the airport, and only using Uber once you're in Sayulita.
